Department Divisions

Explore the specialized divisions available within Leon County Roleplay.

🕵️ CID

Criminal Investigations Division

CID handles complex investigations that require advanced detective work.

Responsibilities include:

• Major crime investigations
• Evidence collection
• Interviews and interrogations
• Case development
• Surveillance operations
• Working with prosecutors

CID members focus on solving long-term cases and bringing organized criminals to justice.

🔥 H.E.A.T

High Enforcement Action Team

H.E.A.T is a specialized enforcement unit designed to handle high-risk operations.

Responsibilities include:

• High-risk arrests
• Tactical operations
• Violent offender apprehension
• Organized crime operations
• Support for patrol units

H.E.A.T members are highly trained officers who respond to dangerous situations.

🚨 Gang & Narcotics

The Gang & Narcotics Division focuses on organized criminal activity and illegal drug investigations.

Responsibilities include:

• Gang investigations
• Undercover operations
• Criminal organization tracking
• Intelligence gathering
• Coordinated enforcement operations

This division works to reduce organized crime throughout Leon County.

🚑 FMO

Field Medical Officer

FMO provides advanced medical support during critical incidents.

Responsibilities include:

• Emergency medical response
• Officer support during dangerous scenes
• Tactical medical assistance
• Patient assessment
• Emergency treatment coordination

FMOs work closely with Fire, EMS, and Law Enforcement.

🚦 Traffic Unit

The Traffic Unit specializes in enforcing traffic laws and improving roadway safety.

Responsibilities include:

• Traffic enforcement
• Accident investigations
• DUI enforcement
• Highway safety operations
• Vehicle inspections

Traffic officers help maintain safe roads throughout Leon County.

🏍️ Motor Unit

The Motor Unit is a specialized motorcycle division focused on traffic enforcement and rapid response.

Responsibilities include:

• Motorcycle patrols
• Traffic enforcement
• Escort operations
• Special events
• Pursuit assistance

Motor officers provide increased mobility and specialized roadway enforcement.
