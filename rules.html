Leon County RP Rules

Please read and understand all rules before joining the server. Failure to follow these rules may result in disciplinary action.

📌 General Rules

• Respect all members of the community.

• No racism, discrimination, harassment, or hate speech.

• Follow staff instructions at all times.

• Do not exploit, glitch, or abuse game mechanics.

• No advertising other communities without permission.

🎭 Roleplay Rules

• Stay in character while actively roleplaying.

• No RDM (Random Deathmatch).

• No VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch).

• No FailRP.

• Create realistic and enjoyable scenarios.

🚔 Law Enforcement Rules

• Follow department policies and SOPs.

• Respect the chain of command.

• Use proper radio communication.

• Do not abuse department equipment.

• Maintain professionalism while on duty.

🚒 Fire & EMS Rules

• Prioritize life safety.

• Maintain professional emergency response.

• Communicate with other departments.

• Do not interfere with active scenes.

• Follow department procedures.

🚗 Civilian Rules

• Civilians must provide realistic roleplay.

• No intentionally ruining scenes.

• Follow traffic laws during normal gameplay.

• Respect emergency services.

• Participate in quality roleplay.

⚖️ Punishment System

Violations may result in:

• Verbal Warning
• Kick
• Temporary Ban
• Permanent Ban

Staff may adjust punishments depending on severity.
